Marmite Spaghetti

Description

Recipe for when you're feeling extra lazy.

Tastes better than it sounds, I swear.

Ingredients

  • 100-150 grams of spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp marmite
  • 50 grams butter
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • grated cheese of your liking to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti al-dente (7-9 min depending on brand)
  2. When pasta is almost cooked, melt the butter in a saucepan, add the minced garlic and let it stir-fry for about 30 seconds. Drain the pasta, reserve 1/2 cup of water it's been cooking in.
  3. Add marmite and couple of tablespoons of reserved pasta water, mix the sauce well. Toss in your cooked spaghetti.
  4. Serve with cheese!
